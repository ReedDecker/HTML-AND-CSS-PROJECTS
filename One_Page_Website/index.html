       History of Constantinople...

          Constantinople is an ancient city in modern-day Turkey that’s now known as Istanbul. First settled in the seventh century B.C.,Constantinople developed into
          a thriving port thanks to its prime geographic location between Europe and Asia and its natural harbor. In A.D. 330, it became the site of Roman Emperor
          Constantine’s “New Rome,”a Christian city of immense wealth and magnificent architecture.
